Siddhanath Kriya Yoga

Your Personal Kriya Yoga Practice Companion

What This Extension Does

Siddhanath Kriya Yoga is a Chrome extension that helps you maintain a consistent Kriya Yoga practice with guided sessions, events, and community features inspired by the teachings of Yogiraj Siddhanath.

Features

  • Belt Progression - 9 belt levels with 280 guided YouTube video sessions
  • Practice Timer - Customizable Kriya Yoga sequence with countdown timer
  • Sacred Sayings - 4,941 spiritual quotes from Yogiraj Siddhanath
  • Events Calendar - Retreats, pilgrimages, and full moon meditations
  • Ashram Sangha - Live community feed for practitioners
  • AI Assistant - Ask questions about the app and get instant help

Getting Help

If you need help using the extension:

  • Click the "? Help" button in the extension header for a full guide
  • Use the AI Chat button (bottom-right) to ask questions in real time
  • Use the "Report Issue" button inside the AI chat to report problems

Troubleshooting

  • Extension not loading? Try disabling and re-enabling it in chrome://extensions
  • Videos not playing? Make sure you're connected to the internet
  • Timer sounds not working? Check your browser's audio permissions
  • Community feed empty? Join the Sangha by entering your name in the community section
