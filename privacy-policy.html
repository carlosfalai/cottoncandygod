Privacy Policy

Siddhanath Kriya Yoga Chrome Extension

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data. Your practice progress stays on your device. Community features require a name. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Stored Locally (on your device only):

  • Practice progress and belt completion status
  • Practice timer settings and technique preferences
  • Billboard messages you customize
  • Extension preferences and settings

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your device unless you use community features.

Stored on our servers (only if you use community features):

  • Display name (when you join the Ashram Sangha community)
  • Community posts, reactions, and comments
  • Support tickets (if you report an issue via the AI assistant)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Local data is used solely to save your practice progress and preferences
  • Community data is used to display the Sangha feed and enable interaction between practitioners
  • Support tickets are used to improve the extension and resolve issues
  • AI chat conversations are processed by Anthropic's Claude API to provide assistance and are not stored permanently

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your data may be processed by:

  • Supabase - Database hosting for community features (name, posts)
  • Anthropic - AI chat processing (conversations are not stored by them beyond the session)
  • Cloudflare - Notification delivery for support tickets

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Server-side data is stored in secure, managed databases
  • API endpoints use rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • No passwords or financial information is collected

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your local data through Chrome's developer tools
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data. Contact us to remove community data.
  • Opt-out: Community features are optional. The extension works fully without joining the Sangha.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for spiritual practitioners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Community features require only a display name.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion:
